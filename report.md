📊 Marketing Campaign Effectiveness Study
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance to identify key drivers of conversion rates and return on investment (ROI). The objective is to evaluate different campaign types and channels, validate assumptions using statistical analysis, and provide data-driven business recommendations.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze campaign performance metrics

Identify trends and relationships in marketing data

Validate assumptions using hypothesis testing

Perform regression analysis

Provide actionable business recommendations

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

Python

Pandas – Data manipulation

NumPy – Numerical operations

Matplotlib & Seaborn – Data visualization

SciPy – Hypothesis testing

Statsmodels – Regression analysis

📂 Project Workflow
1️⃣ Data Loading & Cleaning

Loaded marketing dataset

Handled missing values

Cleaned acquisition cost column

Verified data types

2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Conversion Rate distribution

ROI distribution

Campaign Type vs ROI comparison

Channel performance analysis

Correlation heatmap

3️⃣ Statistical Validation

Conducted T-test to compare campaign performance

Evaluated statistical significance (p-value)

4️⃣ Regression Analysis

Analyzed impact of Engagement Score on Conversion Rate

Interpreted R-squared and coefficient values

📈 Key Insights

Influencer campaigns demonstrate higher median ROI compared to other campaign types.

Google Ads and YouTube show stronger conversion performance.

Engagement Score has a positive relationship with Conversion Rate.

ROI variability suggests differences in strategy effectiveness.

💡 Business Recommendations

Increase investment in high-performing campaign types.

Prioritize high-conversion digital channels.

Enhance engagement strategies to improve conversion outcomes.

Optimize underperforming campaigns to improve cost efficiency.

📌 Conclusion

The analysis demonstrates that campaign type, channel selection, and engagement levels significantly influence marketing performance. Data-driven allocation of marketing budgets can enhance overall return on investment and campaign effectiveness.
